How it all began....

With a splash of paint, some dog-themed decorations, and Max by her side in a dapper bowtie, "Paws and Whiskers Grooming" was born. Flyers were handed out with treats, and soon, dogs of all shapes and sizes were lining up for Lucy's special touch.
